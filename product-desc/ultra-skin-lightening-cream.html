Ultra Skin Lightening Cream

599

This Professional formulation for skinlightining quickly fades away dark spots, Age spots, Acne marks, Melasma, hyperpigmentation, uneven skin tone and Freckles.

Active composition

Niacinamide, Ethyl Ascorbic Acid, Tocopheryl Acetate, Glycyrrhiza Glabra (Licorice) root extract, Glycerin, Caprylic/Capric triglycerides, Olea Europaea (olive) oil, Jojoba Seed Oil

Indication:

FOR SKIN LIGHTENING

How to use:

Apply a small amount of cream all over face, gently massage with upward motion until the cream has been completely absorbed. Make sure the skin is clean and dry before application.

Cautions:

* For external use only.
* Avoid contact with eyes, if contact occurs rinse eyes thoroughly with water.
* If skin irritation occurs stop usage.
* Keep out of reach of children.
* Store below 25 °c do not freeze
