Syndet Face Cleanser

499

This syndet face cleanser is high tolerance cleansing gel with a soap-free, fragrance free and paraben free formula designed for normal to dry skin. It neutralizes the effects of hard water and helps to restore the moisture of skin barrier.

Active composition

Cocamidopropyl Betaine, Potassium Cocoyl Glycinate, Decyl glucoside, Lauryl glucoside, Cocamide DEA, Tea-Cocoyl Glutamate, Potassium Cocoate, Tocopherol.

Indication:

FOR NORMAL TO DRY SKIN

How to use:

Squeeze out required quantity of cleanser on palm, massage gently on your face and neck with an upward circular motion, rinse off with clear water and pat dry.

Cautions:

* For external use only.
* Avoid contact with eyes, if contact occurs rinse eyes thoroughly with water.
* If skin irritation occurs stop usage.
* Keep out of reach of children.
* store at room temperatures. Keep away from direct sunlight.
