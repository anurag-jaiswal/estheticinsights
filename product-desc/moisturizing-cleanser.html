Moisturizing Cleanser

499

Blended with finely milled colloidal oat meal along with other moisturizing agents, specially made for dry to extra dry skin. Cleanser gently buffs away impurities and dead skin. This creamy formula is packed with anti-oxidants to reduce the appearance of ageing.

Active composition

Colloidal oatmeal, Lipids, Beta-glucan, Antioxidants, Syndets, Glycerine, Sorbitol, HYDROXYPROPYL STARCH PHOSPHATE

Indication:

FOR DRY SKIN

How to use:

Wet face with water and massage a small amount of cleanser onto skin, rinse thoroughly with water and pat dry.

Cautions:

* For external use only.
* Avoid contact with eyes, if contact occurs rinse eyes thoroughly with water.
* If skin irritation occurs stop usage.
* Keep out of reach of children.
* store at room temperatures. Keep away from direct sunlight.
