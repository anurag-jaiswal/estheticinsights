In the process of assessing cosmetic needs and preferences of our clients, EI closely watches the global trends and advances in cosmetic technologies and works in faster-modern ways to attain simple & advanced solutions to our client’s needs. Customisation of Cosmetics & Medico-aesthetics at minimum MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity).


Innovative new formulations – Complete support of R&D by International facilities.

Huge packaging options.


Help with Regulations and Registrations.
