You Dream .. We Develop


In the process of assessing cosmetic needs and preferences of our clients, EI closely watches the global trends and advances in cosmetic technologies and works in faster-modern ways to attain simple & advanced solutions to our client’s needs. We develop, manufacture and pack world class cosmetic formulations according to our client’s needs.

Objectives:

• Customisation of Cosmetics & Medico-aesthetics at minimum MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity).
• Innovative new formulations – Complete support of R&D by International facilities.
• QA & QC support for developed formulations.
• Assistance of Personal designer for artworks such as Logo, Label and Cartons.
• Huge packaging options.
• Help with Regulations and Registrations.
• Marketing literature or content.
• Quick delivery.
• Fair Trade/Price to support own brands by Professionals & budding companies.

In order to simplify the process, ‘PRO’ces is divided based on availability of formulations.
1. ‘PRO’ces for Existing Formulation
2. ‘PRO’ces for New Developments




For Existing Formulations:


Objectives:

• MOQ even at two digit numbers.
• Explores best available packaging options.
• Artworks and Designs by professional personal designer.
• Speed delivery.
• Brand Registration & Licensing.
• Fair Trade to support own brands by new companies & Professionals.

For New Developments


Objectives:

• MOQ at lowest possible.
• Complete support of International collaborative R&D facilities from Japan, Taiwan, Russia & India.
• Extensive iterations and Sampling to achieve desired formulation.
• Innovative Packaging options in accordance with new developments.
• QA & QC support to analyse formulation.
• Artworks and Designs by professional personal designer.
• Delivery within timelines.
• Help with new Brand Registration & Licensing.
• Fair Trade to support own brands by new companies & Professionals.

R&D

‘PRO’ces for New developments starts with R&D, as it consumes fair amount of time, it is stated that for new developments, ‘PRO’ces usually requires 3-6 months to finalise formulations and to select among packaging options.

R&D Formulation: It includes development of formula, Multiple Iterations, Sampling & Stabilisation of formula.
R&D Packaging: Innovative new packaging options for new developments are also explored and are finalised by the end of this step.
